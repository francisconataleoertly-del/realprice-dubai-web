# FonatProp Cinematic Ad Production Pack

Date: 2026-04-26
Goal: create a premium AI-generated commercial for a broker/investor meeting, focused only on valuation and the embeddable lead-capture widget.

## Reference DNA

The strongest reference is the horizontal DolarApp-style video:

- A calm premium protagonist in a city/lifestyle setting.
- A surreal money/data metaphor made physical in the real world.
- Cinematic close-ups, shallow depth of field, controlled movement.
- Product appears on a phone, but the emotional hook comes before the UI.
- The ending is simple: brand mark, one promise, one clear action.

For FonatProp, do not copy the dollars idea literally. Translate the same structure:

- DolarApp metaphor: money physically floats through the world.
- FonatProp metaphor: Dubai property values, transactions, and data points become visible around buildings.
- Emotional question: "What is my property really worth?"
- Business answer: "FonatProp turns valuation curiosity into qualified seller leads."

## Recommended Tool Workflow

Use Sora 2 for the cinematic story shots. Use Runway for controlled image-to-video, extensions, and final polish. Use Seedance 2.0 only as an alternate generator for surreal transition shots if Sora does not nail them.

Important: do not ask AI video tools to generate readable UI text. Generate the cinematic footage with blank/abstract phone screens, then overlay real FonatProp screenshots, screen recordings, or clean UI mockups in the editor. This is the difference between "AI toy" and "agency ad."

## Editing Rules

- Add all readable text in post-production, not inside AI generation.
- Keep color palette close to FonatProp: black, white, deep blue, subtle gold sunlight.
- Use English voiceover with Arabic subtitles for the meeting.
- Keep the pacing expensive: fewer cuts, slower camera moves, no TikTok chaos.
- Use real product captures from `https://fonatprop.com/broker-demo` for the valuation and widget moments.
- Make the UI shots short and clear. The film sells the outcome, not the interface.

## Music Direction
