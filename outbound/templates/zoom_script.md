# FonatProp 15-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00-2:00 Discovery

Ask:

- How do you currently value a property before speaking with an owner?
- How long does it take your team to prepare a price opinion?
- Does your website capture seller/owner leads or mostly browsing traffic?

## 2:00-5:00 Pain

Say:

```text
Most agencies have two problems: valuations are slow and subjective, and website visitors often leave without becoming a qualified lead.
```

## 5:00-8:00 Product 1: Valuation

Show:

- AI-assisted valuation.
- Confidence range.
- Evidence and comparables.
- Broker-controlled final conversation.

## 8:00-11:00 Product 2: Widget

Show:

- Visitor enters address/property intent.
- Widget captures name, phone, email.
- Visitor gets a broad range.
- Agency receives a qualified lead.

## 11:00-13:00 Commercial Case

Explain:

```text
FonatProp is not trying to replace the broker. It gives the broker a faster evidence-backed starting point and turns more website traffic into conversations.
```

## 13:00-15:00 Close

Ask:

```text
If we installed this on one page of your website as a pilot, who else would need to approve it?
```
